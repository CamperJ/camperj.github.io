Introduction

Although previously a faithful believer, it is now the position of the author that 'God' as commonly described (per the below) is incompatible with reality, and thus impossible.
The purpose of this page is to lay out the reasoning for such a claim.

Definition

Assuming 'God,' the Creator of 'Creation,' is defined as:
  1. Omnipotent
  2. Omniscient
  3. Omnibenevolent

Questions

  1. If God is omniscient, then was God aware of the snake in the Garden of Eden before it tempted Adam and Eve?
    If so, then why did God not remove the snake from the Garden of Eden?
    If the Garden of Eden story is merely metaphorical, then where does Original Sin come from?
