Akrasia?

Akrasia has been described as "weakness of will." It is that feeling we humans get when procrastinating, and knowing that we could be spending our time more productively. For more information, please refer to akrasia's article on Wikipedia.

Why Akrasia.xyz?

It's an interesting concept that I first read about in Josh Kaufman's The Personal MBA, although I've certainly felt and experienced it before I learned of the term. Part of trying to be a better person and contributing to humanity as a whole is struggling with and overcoming akrasia. So in a sense, it is a powerful reminder.

One of the sub-goals of this site is to find ways to effectively combat akrasia.
