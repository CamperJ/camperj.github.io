Basics

Required materials:

  • Resume/CV
  • Cover letter (sometimes optional, but generally good to have)
  • List of References
Strategy:
Job offers = Applications * (HR screen pass rate) * (Interviews pass rate) ^ (n interview rounds) * (references pass rate) * (background check pass rate)
